AN unforgettable
Dance COMPETITION
Ultra Dance Competition is the most electrifying and innovative dance competition in the industry. With clear, transparent scoring, performers receive instantaneous results and recognition. Ultra provides a state-of-the-art experience for all dancers, teachers, choreographers, and parents. Join us as we continue to innovate while maintaining our dedication to the values and practices that have made this industry what it is today. At Ultra, we want your dancers to be the artists and athletes they were born to be!
ULTRA’s Competition is a held in a fun, supportive environment and adjudicated by our resident judges and special guest judges. Here are just a few of the things that set ULTRA’s Competition apart:
INSTANT & TRANSPARENT SCORING
Just seconds after the conclusion of every performance, dancers and audience members can look at the screens located on the side of our stage to see each performance’s total composite score. Additionally, stay up to date on the current Highest Scoring Overall Performances for the present division by viewing the screens at any time. Feature only available in our Ultra Competitive Level.
STATE-OF -THE-ART- PRODUCTION
At Ultra, you won’t feel like you are at just another dance competition. With state-of-the-art production, interactive lighting, and special effects, dancers will look and feel like the artists they were born to be. We go above and beyond not only to give your dancers the highest quality photos and videos, but to provide parents, teachers, dancers, and audience members the highest quality experience.
rapid access to photos & videos
At Ultra, receive accelerated access to your dancers’ photos and videos. At the conclusion of each performance, wait no longer than 5 hours to view your dancers' high-quality photos and videos in the Parent Center.
LIVE JUDGES FEEDBACK
Dancers receive personalized, constructive, and effective feedback throughout their performance from top tier and well-rounded judges who work in the dance industry. Additionally, each of the judges has the opportunity to press our “Power Performance” button. When a judge presses their “ Power Performance ” button this allows the judge to give live commentary to the dancers on stage at the conclusion of their performance.
frontrow video angle
The NEW FRONTROW Video Angle is an intimate up-close, front-and-center video of your dancer’s performances. A great way to see what the judges see!
DYNAMIC TAP MICROPHONES
At Ultra, we want to hear your tap sounds loud and clear! Our judges and audience members will hear every shuffle and pull back with our mic’d stages for our tap and clogging routines.
EASY ONLINE REGISTRATION
Everything related to our competition can be easily accomplished through your personalized Studio Center account: Easily copy your registration from any KAR or Rainbow Competition PLUS explore your video critiques, score sheets, music uploading, results & more!
