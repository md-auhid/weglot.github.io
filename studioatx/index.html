S3 Charitable Partner Spotlight
We are so excited to have the Human Rights Campaign (HRC) as our charitable partner for Pride 2023. With chapters all over the US, HRC is a great way to get involved with and support our local Chicago and Austin LGBTQ+ communities.
HRC strives to end discrimination against LGBTQ+ people and realize a world that achieves fundamental fairness and equality for all. A mission that Studio Three supports and aims to reflect in the inclusivity of our studios and community.
