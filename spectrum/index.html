The First Word
Welcome to Spotlight, Spectrum’s first newsletter covering updates on products, markets, trends and regulations – in a bitesize format.
Just when we thought most of the surprises were left behind last year, we were reminded that the notion of change will inevitably remain with us much longer. The recent volatility of trading volumes where retail traders took on the goliath institutions was one of those reminders to recognise and embrace new opportunity and possibilities.
On that note we are welcoming this new chapter as we cast a light on the influences, trends and topics that we think will change the course of the year.
As always, we’d welcome your feedback and suggestions. Together, we will shape a better way for retail trading.
Martina Hoffard
Head of Marketing
